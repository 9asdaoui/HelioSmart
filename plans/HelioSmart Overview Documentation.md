# HelioSmart - Solar Energy Estimation Platform

## Overview

HelioSmart is an intelligent solar energy estimation platform designed to help users transition to renewable energy with confidence. The platform combines advanced satellite imagery analysis, weather data, and sophisticated calculations to provide accurate solar installation estimates.

### What Does HelioSmart Do?

At its core, HelioSmart answers one simple question: **"How much solar energy can I generate, and what will it cost me?"**

The platform accomplishes this through several key capabilities:

#### 1. **Roof Analysis & Segmentation**
HelioSmart analyzes satellite or drone imagery of your property to identify usable roof areas. Using advanced AI models, it detects roof boundaries, identifies obstacles like chimneys and vents, and calculates the available space for solar panels.

#### 2. **Solar Potential Calculation**
By integrating with NASA's solar irradiance databases and weather APIs, HelioSmart determines how much sunlight your location receives throughout the year. This includes accounting for seasonal variations, local weather patterns, and shading factors.

#### 3. **System Design & Optimization**
The platform automatically recommends optimal solar panel configurations based on:
- Available roof space
- Local energy prices
- Sun exposure patterns
- Panel efficiency ratings
- Inverter compatibility

#### 4. **Financial Analysis**
HelioSmart provides comprehensive financial projections including:
- Total system cost estimation
- Monthly and annual energy savings
- Payback period calculation
- Return on investment (ROI)
- Long-term savings over 25+ years

#### 5. **Technical Specifications**
The platform generates detailed technical reports with:
- Bill of materials (panels, inverters, wiring)
- Electrical diagrams
- Structural requirements
- Installation guidelines
